TechConnect Africa - Site Plan

1. Site Name

TechConnect Africa — This name represents a community-driven platform to connect African tech enthusiasts, startups, and professionals. The name was chosen to reflect the purpose of building a stronger digital ecosystem in Africa.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide African tech professionals and startups with a platform to:

  • Share news, events, and job opportunities in the tech space.
  • Connect with like-minded individuals through community profiles and discussion forums.
  • Promote tech education through free resources and mentorship programs.

3. Scenarios

  • How can I find African tech events or meetups happening this month?
  • Where can I connect with mentors or startup investors in my country?

4. Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Deep Blue (#1e3a8a) – used for headings and main navigation.
  • Accent Color: Amber (#f59e0b) – used for highlights, buttons, and call-to-actions.
  • Background Color: Light Gray (#f0f4f8) – used for body background for readability.

5. Typography

  • Playfair Display – for headings and the site title to give a bold and elegant impression.
  • Roboto – for body text to ensure clean and easy readability.
